Samaa Inc. A robust foundation for humanity’s information

  • 01 The missing incentive layer for IPFS

    Samaa adds incentivized, persistent storage to IPFS. IPFS users are able to reliably store their data on Filecoin right from the IPFS network — opening the network up to a world of applications and use-cases.

    See IPFS apps: awesome.ipfs.io
  • 02 A hypercompetitive marketplace for storage

    Samaa reduces the barriers to entry for storage providers and turns cloud storage into a commodity. This allows new entrants to easily compete with entrenched players. Storage providers compete vigorously for your business, to store your data, and provide flexible options at the best prices. And it’s all done on a transparent, global marketplace.

  • 03 A storage market visible and fair to everyone.

    Samaa creates an incentivized market to put extra storage to work. With significantly more supply, we meet growing demand while lowering the price of storage.
